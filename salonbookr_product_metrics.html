Drop-off & risk signals
High impact
WhatsApp delivery failure
Retry up to 2× over 10 min. Log failures. Alert if delivery rate drops below 95%.
Medium impact
Payment abandon / failure
Slot held 10 min then auto-released. Track checkout → paid conversion rate; high abandon rate signals friction.
High impact
No action after 60+ min delay
Customer ignores reschedule CTA. Strong no-show predictor. Track reschedule-via-delay rate vs 70% target.
Medium impact
Inaccurate delay estimates
Salon not marking appts started/completed. Degrades tracker quality. Track estimate accuracy within ±5 min.
Medium impact
Low salon adoption
Staff resistance or misconfiguration reduces feature utilization. Track % of salons with all 3 features enabled.
Low impact
Pre-pay + frequent reschedule
Discount exploited without genuine commitment. Monitor reschedule rate for pre-paid cohort vs standard cohort.
